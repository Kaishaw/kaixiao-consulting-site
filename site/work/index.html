Public sector Dynamics 365 cloud migration

Moving a legacy public-sector CRM into the Microsoft cloud

On a provincial public-sector project, I worked as a Functional Consultant helping migrate an older on-premises Dynamics 365 environment to the cloud. The system supported more than 500 users and had years of custom plugins, workflows, and performance issues.

My role was to connect business requirements with technical delivery: analyzing gaps, working with developers on more than 30 legacy plugin rewrites, replacing old workflows with Power Automate, and coordinating UAT with business and security teams.

Outcome: zero rollback go-live, roughly 50% faster page loads, and a more stable platform ready for future Dynamics 365 and Power Platform upgrades.

Food distribution CRM + ERP transformation

Modernizing operations for a Canadian Asian food manufacturer

For a growing Asian food business in Canada, the challenge was practical and urgent: perishable products, many SKUs, import and repack workflows, and sales teams needing faster inventory and customer insight.

I supported CRM requirements and process design while working closely with Business Central, Power Apps, data, and BI workstreams. One important piece was a replenishment app that helped feed live operational data into dashboards for better decisions.

Outcome: less manual entry, better stock visibility, faster forecasting, and more time for sales teams to focus on key grocery and hospitality accounts.

Enterprise manufacturing Large-scale D365 rollout

Consolidating fragmented systems for a top-three Chinese baijiu group

One of my largest projects involved a major Chinese baijiu manufacturing and sales group with multiple production bases, thousands of distributors, and millions of online customers. The organization had separate systems for production, product, sales, service, finance, and warehousing.

As a lead business systems analyst, I helped map requirements across CRM, Business Central, F&O, data warehouse, and BI workstreams. The work included product management, customer service, marketing, procurement, finance, and adoption planning across a large stakeholder group.

Outcome: five legacy systems retired, better supply-chain visibility, faster reporting, and stronger adoption through role-based training and pilot sessions.

Sales & marketing Lead scoring automation

Helping sales managers spend less time sorting leads manually

In a sales and marketing project, workshops revealed that managers were spending too much time reviewing and assigning leads. The work was repetitive, but it affected response time, customer experience, and campaign ROI.

I helped design a segmentation and scoring approach using historical customer data, then connected the scoring logic with Power Automate so different lead levels could be routed to the right sales roles for follow-up.

Outcome: faster follow-up, higher sales team efficiency, better customer satisfaction, and more time for managers to focus on planning instead of manual triage.
